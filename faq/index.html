Waymark

What is Waymark?

Waymark is an iPadOS productivity application that captures spoken content in real-time via live transcription, translates it across 15+ languages, and uses AI to transform meetings and seminars into actionable intelligence. All session data is stored locally on your iPad.

What platforms does Waymark support?

Waymark is designed exclusively for iPad running iPadOS 17.0 or later. It is optimized for landscape orientation and supports Apple Pencil for drawing and annotation. Waymark is not available on iPhone, Mac, or other platforms.

Does Waymark require an internet connection?

An internet connection is required for live transcription, translation, AI analysis, and account authentication. You can review existing sessions, edit notes, and browse your session list offline.

How many languages does Waymark support?

Waymark supports transcription in 15 languages and translation across 13 language pairs. The app interface is available in English, Korean, and Chinese. See the Waymark product page for the full list.

Is my Waymark data stored on your servers?

No. All session data (transcripts, translations, notes, drawings, photos, and AI reports) is stored exclusively on your iPad. We never store your session content on our servers. When you use cloud features like transcription or AI analysis, data is transmitted transiently for processing but is not retained. See the Waymark Privacy Policy for details.

Does Waymark require a subscription?

No. Waymark uses a pay-as-you-go credit system with no subscriptions. You purchase credit packs only when you need them. Each credit pack expires one month after purchase. New accounts receive a one-time free allocation to get started.

Where can I get support for Waymark?

Visit the Waymark Support page for detailed FAQs, troubleshooting guides, and how-to articles. You can also contact us directly at support@wtheory.com.

Access Canada

What is Access Canada?

Access Canada is a strategic insights platform for businesses seeking to enter or expand within the Canadian market. It helps uncover federal grants, government tenders, optimal sales channels, and connects enterprises directly with Canadian capital, angel investors, and B2B buyers.

Who is Access Canada designed for?

Access Canada is designed for both foreign enterprises looking to enter the Canadian market and local Canadian businesses seeking to expand their reach, access government funding, and connect with new sales channels.

When will Access Canada be available?

Access Canada is currently in development. Visit the Access Canada page for the latest updates or contact us at support@wtheory.com to express interest.

What kind of grants and programs does Access Canada cover?

Access Canada aims to provide comprehensive coverage of federal and provincial grants, government tender opportunities, tax incentive programs, industry-specific subsidies, and other public funding mechanisms available to businesses operating in Canada.

Signet

What is Signet?

Signet is a local marketing and customer acquisition platform that connects businesses with their immediate community through a frictionless free-sampling marketplace. Businesses can showcase classes, products, and services while users discover and try offerings from local providers risk-free.

How does Signet help business owners?

Signet serves as a highly targeted marketing engine that drives foot traffic and product trials. Instead of traditional advertising, business owners can offer free samples, trial classes, or introductory experiences directly to consumers in their local area, converting them into loyal customers.

When will Signet be available?

Signet is currently in development. Visit the Signet page for the latest updates or contact us at support@wtheory.com to express interest.

Is Signet free for consumers?

Signet is designed as a two-sided marketplace. Consumers can discover and try free samples and introductory offerings at no cost. Business owners use the platform to reach their target local audience through paid promotional placements.

Privacy & Data

Does the company sell my personal data?

No. We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. This applies to both our website and all of our applications. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

What data does the wtheory.com website collect?

The website collects minimal data: standard web server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) and basic analytics cookies for aggregate usage statistics. We do not require account creation or collect financial information on the website. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Does the website use cookies?

Yes, the website uses a minimal number of cookies for basic functionality and aggregate analytics. We do not use advertising, targeting, or social media tracking cookies. See our Cookies Policy for details.

Billing & Payments

How are payments processed?

For our mobile applications (Waymark, Access Canada, Signet), all payments are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not directly collect or store your payment information, credit card numbers, or billing address. Web development services are billed separately. Contact us for details.

How do I request a refund for an app purchase?

Since all app purchases are processed by Apple, refunds are governed by Apple's refund policies. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com or contact Apple Support directly.

Do any of the apps have subscription fees?

No. None of our mobile applications use subscription billing. Waymark uses a pay-as-you-go credit system where you purchase credit packs only when needed. Pricing models for other apps are detailed on their respective product pages.
