Security & Privacy

Your data stays yours.
No exceptions.

Everything you record, write, draw, and photograph in Waymark is stored only on your iPad. We never see your content, never store it on our servers, and never share it with anyone.

Your Content Never Leaves Your iPad

Transcripts, notes, drawings, photos, and reports are saved directly to your device. No cloud sync, no server copies. If you delete it, it's gone.

Audio Is Never Recorded

Your voice is processed in real time and immediately discarded. No audio file is ever created, stored, or accessible to us or any third party.

You Control When Data Is Shared

Nothing is sent to the cloud unless you choose to. Transcription, translation, and analysis only happen when you tap the button. You decide what gets processed and when.

No Tracking, No Ads, No Third Parties

Waymark contains zero third-party SDKs, analytics trackers, or advertising code. The app is built entirely with Apple's own frameworks. Your usage data stays private.

Sign In Securely with Apple

Your account is protected by Apple's identity system. We never see your password, and you can use a private relay email so your real address stays hidden.

Delete Everything, Anytime

Delete your account from Settings and all server-side data is permanently removed on the spot. No waiting period, no hidden backups, no questions asked.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for any purpose. Your content belongs to you.

FAQ

Questions? Answered.

What is Waymark?

Waymark is an iPadOS productivity app designed for seminars, lectures, meetings, and any scenario where you need to capture spoken content. It combines live speech-to-text transcription, real-time translation, rich note-taking with drawing and photo annotation, AI-powered session analysis, and PDF export — all in one app.

What devices does Waymark support?

Waymark is designed exclusively for iPad running iPadOS 17.0 or later. It is optimized for landscape orientation and supports Apple Pencil for drawing and annotation. Waymark is not available on iPhone, Mac, or other platforms.

Do I need an internet connection?

An internet connection is required for live transcription, translation, AI analysis, and purchasing credit packs. You can review existing sessions, edit notes, and browse your session list offline.

Is there a subscription?

No. Waymark uses a pay-as-you-go model with consumable credit packs. There are no subscriptions, no auto-renewals, and no recurring charges. You purchase credits only when you need them.

What do I get for free?

Every new account receives a one-time free allocation of transcription minutes, translation minutes, and AI analysis credits. This allocation expires 30 days after signup. All features are available — no artificial restrictions on free accounts except the watermark on PDF exports and a 50-session lifetime cap.

Do purchased credits expire?

Yes. Each credit pack expires 1 month after the date of purchase. Credits are consumed on a first-in, first-out (FIFO) basis — the oldest credits are used first. Expired, unused credits are forfeited and cannot be refunded.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes. All session content — transcripts, translations, notes, drawings, photos, and AI reports — is stored exclusively on your iPad. We never store your content on our servers. Audio is processed in real time and is not stored anywhere after transcription. All API communication is encrypted and cryptographically signed using Apple's App Attest.

Is my audio recorded and stored on your servers?

No. Audio is streamed to our speech-to-text provider in real time during recording and is not stored — not on our servers, and not by the provider after processing. We never have access to your raw audio.

When is my data sent to AI?

Your session data is only sent when you explicitly tap the "Analyze" button. It is never sent automatically. You are always in control of when and what is shared with the AI service. The data sent includes your transcript text, notes text, and up to 5 photos from the session.

Can I use Waymark on multiple iPads?

Yes. Sign in with the same Apple ID on any iPad and your credit balance syncs. However, session content is stored locally and does not sync between devices — each device has its own session library.

What happens if I delete the app?

Uninstalling Waymark permanently deletes all session data from your iPad. This data cannot be recovered. Your server-side account (credit balances, purchase history) remains intact and will be available when you reinstall and sign in again.

How do I request a refund?

All purchases are processed by Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com or contact Apple Support. We do not process refunds directly.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Delete Account within the app. This immediately and permanently deletes all your server-side data, including authentication records, credit balances, purchase history, usage logs, and security keys.
